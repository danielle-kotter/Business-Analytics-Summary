Chapter - 2 Basics

variable <- 1:10

Mathmatical values

\(\mu\) = Population mean
\(\sigma\) = Population sd
\(\bar{x}\) = Sample mean
\({e}\) = Standard error \(\pi\) = Pie \(\ge\) = Bigger than
\(\le\) = Smaller than

Sample mean, standard deviation

mean(variable)
[1] 5.5
sd(variable)
[1] 3.02765

Removes values NA in a data set:

mean(variable, na.rm = TRUE)
[1] 5.5
sd(variable, na.rm = TRUE)
[1] 3.02765

Weighted mean & standard deviation

library(Hmisc)
weightedmean <- wtd.mean(x,y)
weightedsd <- sqrt(wtd.var(x,y))/sqrt(n)

Variance

var(variable)
[1] 9.166667

Tables frames & Matrixes

As matrix =

library(data.table)
matrix(c(1:8), nrow = 4, byrow = TRUE) #organized by row
     [,1] [,2]
[1,]    1    2
[2,]    3    4
[3,]    5    6
[4,]    7    8
matrix(c(1:8), ncol = 4, byrow = FALSE) #organized by col

[1,]    1    3    5    7
[2,]    2    4    6    8

Transforming tables from to other formats =

table1 <- data.table(matrix(c(1:8), nrow = 4))
as.data.frame(table1)

3  3  7
4  4  8
table2 <- data.frame(Column1 = c(1:5), Column2 = c(1:5))
as.data.table(table2)
   Column1 Column2
1:       1       1
2:       2       2
3:       3       3
4:       4       4
5:       5       5
